How 2 order

Ordering is done by DMing me on discord or bluesky, they are linked in the socials page!

When ordering, please provide the following

  • type of comm (how much of your character do you want drawn, what shading style etc.)
  • Your character ref sheet (or just any refrence you can get)
  • a pose you want me to draw
  • any clothing
  • any background
  • body type prefered (if you dont want the body type in the ref sheet.)
  • Extra instructions

    • Please do not be mysterious or vague when ordering, or else i will get confused and might have to turn down the order due to lack of information

    How 2 pay

    When im done with the sketch AND you are happy with it, you can pay via any payment method stated in the tos.

    I can wait for payment, but please try to pay within one week of confirmation.
